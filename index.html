3. What does your implementation process look like?

Our implementation process is designed to be transparent, efficient, and comprehensive. Here's how we work:

  1. Kick Off Call: We start with a Kick Off Call to map out your plan and objectives. This initial discussion helps us understand your needs and goals.

  2. Direct Communications: We maintain direct communications on Slack, providing you with real-time status updates, Q&A sessions, live demonstrations, and detailed reports. We want to ensure you're informed every step of the way.

  3. Integration of Current Systems: Our team takes care of integrating your current systems seamlessly into our workflow, ensuring a smooth transition.

  4. Scripting: We handle the scripting, ensuring that your campaigns are tailored to your specific requirements and objectives.

  5. Testing: Rigorous testing is an integral part of our process. We thoroughly test all components to ensure they perform optimally.

  6. Campaign Rollout: Once everything is in place, we roll out your campaigns, ensuring that they launch successfully.

  7. Optimization: We continually optimize your campaigns to enhance performance and achieve the best results.

  8. Scaling the Agents: When the time is right, we scale up the number of agents to meet your growing needs and demand.

  9. Script for Transition: As a bonus, we provide you with a script that you can use to gracefully transition away from human setters when the time is right, complete with a touch of humor.

Our implementation process is designed to be a collaborative and smooth experience, and we are here to support you every step of the way.
